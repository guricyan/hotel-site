# Photographer shot list: locality section

The following original photographs will replace the temporary licensed images.

## Coast
- Wide landscape of Cape Kyan or a nearby southern coast.
- View of the sea framed by plants, stone walls or a narrow path.
- Reef, shallow water and wave details.
- One soft sunset or late-afternoon image.
- Avoid a generic sightseeing-postcard composition.

## Village
- Quiet lanes in Kyan village.
- Red tile roofs, stone walls, wooden gates, plants and older houses.
- A village road leading toward the sea.
- A modern, calm streetscape in Shiozakicho for contrast.
- Do not show readable private addresses, license plates or identifiable residents without permission.

## Food and market
- Okinawan home-style dishes on one table.
- Goya champuru, Okinawa soba, sea grapes, island tofu and local seafood.
- Fish and produce at Itoman Fish Center and Umanchu Market.
- Hands selecting ingredients or preparing breakfast in a guesthouse kitchen.
- Obtain permission before clearly photographing shop staff or customers.

## Delivery
- Shoot horizontal 4:3 and 3:2 versions.
- Long edge at least 3000 px.
- Natural color, no heavy HDR or filters.
- Provide original files without messaging-app compression.
